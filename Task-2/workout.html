Workout Plans

Beginner Plan

This plan is designed for beginners who are just starting their fitness journey. It includes basic exercises that help build a strong foundation.

  • Push-ups
  • Squats
  • Plank
  • Jumping Jacks

Intermediate Plan

This plan is for those who have some experience with workouts and are looking to take their fitness to the next level with more challenging exercises.

  • Burpees
  • Lunges
  • Mountain Climbers
  • Bench Press

Advanced Plan

This plan is for advanced fitness enthusiasts who want to push their limits with high-intensity exercises and complex workout routines.

  • Deadlifts
  • Pull-ups
  • Box Jumps
  • Snatches
